Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower every South African household with affordable and efficient solar solutions that provide energy independence and significant cost savings.

We're committed to making solar technology accessible and understandable, helping our customers navigate the complex world of renewable energy with confidence.

Our Vision

To create a South Africa where every home is powered by clean, renewable energy, free from the constraints of load shedding and rising electricity costs.

We envision communities that are energy-resilient, environmentally conscious, and economically empowered through solar technology.

Our Journey

January 2020

The Beginning

Founded in Cape Town by renewable energy engineers who experienced firsthand the challenges of South Africa's energy crisis.

July 2021

First Major Installation

Completed our first large-scale residential solar installation, helping a family of six become completely energy independent.

March 2022

AI Assistant Launch

Introduced our AI-powered energy assistant to help South Africans optimize their solar usage and reduce electricity costs.

Present

Nationwide Impact

Serving thousands of households across all nine provinces, with plans to expand our community solar initiatives.
